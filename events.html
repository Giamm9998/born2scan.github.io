DanteCTF

DanteCTF is our online jeopardy-style CTF with dynamic scoring and a Dantesque theme. The challenges have various difficulty levels; there are some easy ones and some harder ones.

CTFtime CTF page

2023

➤ June 2, 07:00 UTC to June 4, 21:00 UTC

This was our first public event.

It was an online jeopardy-style CTF with dynamic scoring. The target was a medium-level CTF with mainly medium and easy difficulty challenges, and also some harder ones. The CTF run for 62 hours and the categories included crypto, pwn, web, forensics, networking and radio.

2021

➤ June 19, 08:00 UTC to June 19, 20:00 UTC

This was our first trial open only on invitation. It was fundamental to test the platform, infrastructure and tools.

The CTF run for 12 hours and was entirely in Italian, our native language.
